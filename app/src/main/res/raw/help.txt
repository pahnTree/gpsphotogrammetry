Settings Tab:
    GPS
        - Internal GPS will gather data
        - Pixhawk (Optional)
    NOTE: Must have 3DR Services App installed for Pixhawk to work.

Details Tab:
    Start Gathering Data
        - Pressing this button will begin a process to gather GPS location data.
        - The smartphone's internal magnetic sensor, gyroscope, and accelerometer data will also be gathered.
    Stop Gathering Data
        - Ends the above processes.

Camera Tab:
    Timer:
        - Take images at a set interval time for an unlimited number of photos.
    Take Image:
        - Start taking pictures.
        - Similar to a Timelapse feature.
        - Pressing the Take Image button a second time will end the process.
